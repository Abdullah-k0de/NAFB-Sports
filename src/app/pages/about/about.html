Our Story

Founded on the belief that sports and movement belong to everyone, NAFB Sports breaks down the barriers of traditional athletics to build communities of health, confidence, and joy.

Our Mission

To engage and empower all youth, teens, and adults through sport and exercise in a judgment-free, supportive environment that focuses on holistic wellness.

Our Vision

We envision a world where personal health and sports are inclusive, accessible, and completely pressure-free — where every individual can experience the joy of movement and thrive within a supportive community.

How We Started

NAFB Sports was founded to bridge a critical gap in our communities. We saw that traditional sports programs often prioritize the top performers, leaving behind those who just want to move, learn, and have fun without the pressure of competition.

We designed a methodology that strips away the scoreboard and focuses entirely on the joy of movement. By partnering with local clubs, schools, and organizations, we bring our inclusive programs directly to the communities that need them most.

Est. with Purpose

Our Impact

By breaking down sports into fundamental movements, we foster self-esteem, reduce anxiety, and prove that you don't need to be a professional athlete to enjoy the benefits of an active lifestyle.
